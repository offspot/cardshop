Using your hotspot

Once the image has been downloaded, flash it onto a microSD card (we recommend Raspberry Pi Imager, but any such tool will do).

Insert the card into your Raspberry Pi (either 3B+, 4, 5 or 400; the zero 2W is not supported yet), plug it in, and wait for approximately 1-2 minutes during the first boot-up process until you see the network status display Ready (or Wikipedia if you purchased the Wikipedia-only configuration) in your wifi settings.

Connect to it and a captive portal should appear after a few seconds. If not, then open http://goto.kiwix.hotspot in your favourite browser.

Enjoy!
